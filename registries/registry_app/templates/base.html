Over 150 registries and databases from all over Europe come together in our Network.

n-ost is a network for media workers with special focus on Eastern Europe, and for experts on Eastern Europe interested in the media. Our members are:

- Media workers and experts on Eastern Europe,

- Photographers, print and radio journalists,

- Scholars, project managers, trainers, translators, film makers, artists,

- Eastern and Western Europeans who live or have lived in Eastern Europe,

- freelance journalists and other independent contractors, for whom our network serves as a catalyst, platform, springboard, or forum.
